<objective>
Remove the left tab bar offset when the window is in fullscreen mode.

Purpose: On macOS, the tab bar and welcome screen have ~78px left padding to avoid overlapping the window traffic light controls (close/minimize/maximize). When the window enters fullscreen mode (Ctrl+Cmd+F or double-click green button), the traffic lights are hidden by macOS but the padding remains, creating an awkward empty gap. This plan detects fullscreen state and conditionally removes the padding.

Output: Both App.svelte (tab bar) and WelcomeScreen.svelte (drag region) dynamically show/hide left padding based on fullscreen state.
</objective>
